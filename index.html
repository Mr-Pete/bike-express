About Us

Who We Are

Bike Express are a dedicated, professional and friendly team of bicycle couriers. We operate throughout the Greater Dublin Area, offering express delivery, same-day delivery, and scheduled delivery of parcels, emergency items and pharmaceuticals.

Our Values

We aim to deliver positive change one parcel at a time by focusing on change, sustainability, transparency, and our stakeholders. We believe that sustainability can be handled through the complete supply chain. We ship 100% carbon neutral

Our Mission

At Bike Express we take great pride in our standards and work closely with our customers to ensure that requirements and objectives are consistently met. We understand that schedules have to be met and vital, time-critical deliveries made. As such, we operate 24 hours a day, 7 days per week.
